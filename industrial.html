Industrial

SATT Electric LLC offers proven technical and specialized engineering solutions for your most complex industrial electrical challenges.

SATT Electric LLC provides a wide range of industrial electrical services including complete turnkey control systems, systems integration, electric power system studies, onsite power system engineering, as well as design and construction.

Experienced electricians using state-of-the-art equipment and heavy-duty support vehicles provide SATT Electric LLC's industrial clients with high-quality electrical support. Knowledgeable in high-voltage situations, SATT Electric LLC provides power system analysis and studies that include installation, checkout, testing, and fault analysis. Services also include general maintenance and emergency troubleshooting.
