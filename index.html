Industrial

SATT Electric LLC offers proven technical and specialized engineering solutions for your most complex industrial electrical challenges.

SATT Electric LLC provides a wide range of industrial electrical services including complete turnkey control systems, systems integration, electric power system studies, onsite power system engineering, as well as design and construction.

Experienced electricians using state-of-the-art equipment and heavy-duty support vehicles provide SATT Electric LLC's industrial clients with high-quality electrical support. Knowledgeable in high-voltage situations, SATT Electric LLC provides power system analysis and studies that include installation, checkout, testing, and fault analysis. Services also include general maintenance and emergency troubleshooting.

Commercial

SATT Electric LLC delivers comprehensive commercial electrical solutions designed to keep your business operations running efficiently and safely.

Our commercial electrical services encompass everything from new construction and tenant improvements to ongoing maintenance and emergency repairs. We understand the unique demands of commercial properties and provide tailored electrical solutions for office buildings, retail spaces, restaurants, and mixed-use developments.

Our certified electricians specialize in commercial-grade electrical systems, including advanced lighting controls, data and communication systems, backup power solutions, and energy management systems. We work closely with contractors, architects, and property managers to ensure projects are completed on time and within budget while meeting all code requirements.

Institutional

SATT Electric LLC provides specialized electrical services for institutional facilities requiring the highest standards of safety, reliability, and compliance.

We serve a diverse range of institutional clients including healthcare facilities, educational institutions, government buildings, and religious organizations. Our team understands the critical nature of these facilities and the importance of maintaining continuous, reliable electrical service.

Our institutional services include emergency power systems, life safety systems, specialized lighting solutions, and compliance with strict regulatory standards. We have extensive experience with backup generators, uninterruptible power supplies (UPS), fire alarm systems, and security systems. Our electricians are trained in the unique requirements of institutional environments and work efficiently to minimize disruption to daily operations.
